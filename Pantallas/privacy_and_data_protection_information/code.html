Privacy & Data

Trusted Partnership

Your privacy is our priority

Your safety is our priority, and so is your privacy. Here is how we handle your information in partnership with your local municipality and urban safety services.

person_off

No Personal Data

No names or emails are stored on our servers. You remain completely anonymous during your journey.

enhanced_encryption

Encrypted Logs

All navigation data is protected with military-grade encryption. Your route information is for your eyes only.

history_toggle_off

Ephemeral Storage

Journeys are deleted immediately upon arrival. We don't keep a permanent history of your past routes.

gavel

Emergency Use Only

Only the municipality retains the secure key to link anonymous session codes to identities. This is used exclusively in verified emergency situations reported via official channels.
